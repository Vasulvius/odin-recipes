Lasagna Recipe (8 people)

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1l tomato sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 300g Gruyère cheese
  • Olive oil
  • 70g butter
  • 70g flour
  • 50cl milk
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 210°C.
  2. Make the Bolognese sauce:
    1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
    2. Add minced onion and garlic. Cook until softened.
    3. Add tomato sauce. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Make the béchamel sauce:
    1. In a saucepan, melt butter and add flour. Cook for a minute, stirring constantly.
    2. Gradually add milk, whisking vigorously to avoid lumps, until the sauce thickens.
    3. Add nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a baking dish, layer béchamel sauce, Bolognese sauce, and lasagna noodles.
  5. Top with a final layer of béchamel sauce, Bolognese sauce, and Gruyère cheese.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
