Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 150g Guanciale
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, cut guanciale into small strips.
  3. In a pan, fry guanciale over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside, reserving some of the rendered fat.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  5. Drain the spaghetti, reserving about a cup of pasta water.
  6. Add the spaghetti to the pan with the guanciale. Toss to coat.
  7. Remove the pan from the heat. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti, tossing quickly to prevent the eggs from scrambling. Add a little pasta water at a time to create a creamy sauce.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with more Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper.
